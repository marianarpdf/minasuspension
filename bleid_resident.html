BLEID

A supporter of music regardless of genres, BLEID has been getting known for her versatility as both a live act and a DJ, with her sets being meticulous journeys through several speeds and atmospheres, covering a wide range of musical influences all fused together in a dance-floor friendly manner.

Her two releases out so far keep in mind the diversity that can be found both on her live and DJ sets, with her self titled tape BLEID (2017) out on LABAREDA exploring her taste for darker moods and slow building cinematic atmospheres and the EP Badness (2018) with Violet out on naive, a take on dance-floor oriented music and drum heavy sounds.

Involved in some of Lisbon’s most exciting projects, BLEID is a familiar “face” at Príncipe Discos’ club nights, playing around Europe as part of the crew in festivals like Hyperreality (Viena) or Creepe Teepe (Prague). Besides that, she is also a resident at sex-positive techno party mina, one of the founders behind artist collective Intera and hosts her show Dismantle on Rádio Quântica every month.
