ketia

A renaissance girl in her own right, ketia is a DJ, a producer, an activist and a party promoter - and one of the most active new artists on the Lisbon scene. A keen ear for new and exciting music, alongside a curious, invested approach to community-building within the music scene.
Founder and resident of Kit Ket - a collective and a party that prioritises the safety and inclusion of womxn and non-binary people.


Now a resident at mina where she started as coat check bitch and got inspired and pushed to become a DJ due to the hypnotic sounds and landscapes alongside queer and sexual freedom.
