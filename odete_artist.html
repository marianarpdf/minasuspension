ODETE

Odete works between the field of writing, music, performance and visual arts - her work is explicitly autobiographic, making clear the border between the personal and politics.

She describes her music as a sensorial theory for a weary girlhood - a ghost warrior that becomes visible through mixing. Through transitioning. Failure, silence, brokenness and emptiness as the basis of a queer dramaturgy of track making.

Her sets are something between background noises of a funeral recorded through a smartphone mixed with samples of things being destroyed - beats that traverse the history of queer music - vogue claps, sapphic fragments, industrial beats, punk screams.
