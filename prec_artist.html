Prec

Prec flows between DJing and producing always commanded by a desire to obliterate language and fake objectivity through the exploration of dewy loops, textures, rebonds and percussion résonance.

Isolda's host at Rádio Quântica and creator of the homonymous parties, Prec tries to bring experimental and avant-garde soundscapes to the club and rave scene with the hope of breaking through the linear and boring clubbing.
